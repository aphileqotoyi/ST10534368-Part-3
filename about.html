Welcome to Ubuntu Youth Skills Development (UYSD)

Ubuntu Youth Skills Development (UYSD) is an non-profit organization (NPO) established to empower young people between the ages of 18 and 30. The organization focuses on digital literacy training, entrepreneurship development, and career mentorship. It operates within underserved communities and partners with local business and volunteers to deliver practical training programs.

About Us

I have choose to create an imaginary non-profit organization (NPO) called Ubuntu Youth Skills Development (UYSD). This organization focuses on empowering unemployed youth in South Africa by providing digital literacy training, career guidance, and entrepreneurship skills. The word “Ubuntu” reflects the African philosophy of community and shared humanity, which aligns with the organization’s mission to uplift young people and promote collective growth.

Mission

To empower and equip young people with relevant digital, entrepreneurial, and professional skills that enhance employability and promote self-sufficiency.

Vision

To create generation of skilled, confident, and economically active youth who contribute positively to their communities and the broader economy.
