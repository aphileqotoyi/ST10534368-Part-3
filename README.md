Website Choice                                                            
Name: Ubuntu Youth Skills Development
Organisation Description:
Ubuntu Youth Skills Development (UYSD) is an non-profit organization (NPO) established to empower young people between the ages of 18 and 30. The organization focuses on digital literacy training, entrepreneurship development, and career mentorship. It operates within underserved communities and partners with local business and volunteers to deliver practical training programs.

Reason for Choosing the Organisation:

I have choose to create Ubuntu Youth Skills Development (UYSD) because of youth unemployment and lack of access to digital skills remain significant challenges in South Africa. Many young people, particularly those are from disadvantaged communities, struggle to secure employment due to limited practical skills, lack of career guidance, and minimal exposure to technology. I believe that empowering youth through education and skills training is one of the most effective ways to reduce poverty and promote sustainable community development. 
 


Website Goals and Objectives:
The core objectives of UYSD include reducing youth unemployment, promoting digital inclusion, and fostering entrepreneurship. The organization values integrity, inclusivity, community development, empowerment, and lifelong learning. 
The website developed for UYSD will reflect these goals by maintaining a professional, inclusive, and user-friendly design. It will clearly communicate the mission and vision, highlight success stories, and provide accessible website with the organization’s vision, UYSD will strengthen its brand identify maximize its social impact.
